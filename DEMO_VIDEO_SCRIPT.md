2. Take/select a photo of a terracotta pot (even if slightly blurry/poor quality)
3. Record voice message in your regional language (or English with accent):

Main isko 1000 rupaye mein bechna chahta hoon."

**Translation shown as subtitle:**
"Hello, my name is Rajesh. I made this clay pot with 20 years of experience. This is an old tradition of our village. My grandmother also did this work. It takes 3 days to make this pot. It's made from completely natural clay, no chemicals. You can use it at home for storing water or planting flowers. I want to sell it for 1000 rupees."

**Visual:**
- Show sending photo + voice message

## Technical Notes for Recording:

### For WhatsApp Scenes:
1. Use screen recording on phone
2. Ensure good lighting for product photo
3. Record voice message clearly (can add subtitles in editing)
4. Show real-time responses (2-second delay is fine)

### For Website Scenes:
1. Use browser screen recording
2. Show smooth navigation
3. Highlight key features with zoom/annotations
4. Show responsive design (mobile view)

### Audio:
1. Professional voiceover (or your own clear narration)
2. Background music: Subtle, inspiring (Indian classical fusion?)
3. Sound effects: Minimal (message send/receive sounds)

### Editing:
1. Add subtitles for voice messages
2. Highlight key text on screen
3. Use smooth transitions
4. Add animated graphics for "AI processing" visualization
5. Color grade for warm, authentic feel

### Key Message to Emphasize:
- **Not just translation** - It's about preserving and amplifying the artisan's authentic story
- **Not just listing** - It's about cultural storytelling for global audiences
- **Not just technology** - It's about empowerment and inclusion
